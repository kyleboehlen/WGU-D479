What type of power outlets are on the island?

120 volts, same as the United States.

Is alcohol allowed on the island?

Yes, but it cannot be served or purchased between midnight and 9 AM.

What is the drinking age on the island?

The drinking age is 18, however it is not strictly enforced.

Is English spoken on Taniti?

Many younger Tanitians speak very fluent English. However in the rural areas, especially by older Tanitians, very little English is spoken.

Are there medical services on the island?

There is one major hospital and several clinics. The hospital employees are multilingual.

Is there crime on the island?

Violent crime is extremely rare, however as tourism increases there are more reports of petty crime such as pickpocketing.
